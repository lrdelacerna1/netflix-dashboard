Content Volume & Momentum
Movies vs TV Shows Added per Year
Raw annual counts + 3-year centred moving average — 2014 to 2021
Movies peaked in 2019 at 1,424 titles then declined sharply. TV shows grew steadily — their 3-yr MA climbed from 69 (2015) to 564 (2020).
Share Trajectory & Forecast
TV Share Trend & 3-Year Forecast
% of annual new-title additions — historical 2014–2021 + Holt linear forecast 2022–2024
TV share crossed 30% by 2015 and recovered to 34% in 2021. Holt forecast suggests TV may surpass movies in new-title additions by 2023–2024.
Genre Composition
Genre Mix Over Time
Share of annual additions per primary genre — 2014 to 2021 (stacked area, %)
Documentaries dominated in 2014 (61.5%) but fell to ~10% by 2021. Dramas became the largest genre by 2018 and held that position.
Year-on-Year Swing
Year-on-Year TV Share Change
Percentage-point delta in TV's share of new-title additions
2016 was the biggest single-year swing toward TV (+9.3pp). By 2019–2021 the oscillation stabilised, with TV gaining a modest +2–4pp per year.
